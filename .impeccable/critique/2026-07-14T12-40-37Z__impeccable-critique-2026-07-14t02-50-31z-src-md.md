## Anti-pattern verdict

The site does not read as generic AI landing-page output. It has a recognizable Brigada voice: named dogs, real photography, condensed display type and a purposeful cyan/magenta/orange palette. The homepage now meets the earlier plan's essential sequence: named current dog, immediate roster, practical routes and proof.

The remaining risk is over-systematization. Black offset shadows, magenta underlines, uppercase display headings, numerical strips, ruled edges and saturated bands recur so frequently that the pages can feel like one zine/brutalist campaign template rather than distinct journeys. This conflicts with the intended calm, direct and reliable side of the brand and with the supplied mockup's quieter, image-forward confidence.

The deterministic scan returned 20 findings: 4 warnings and 16 advisories. The dynamic lightbox image is a false positive because client code supplies its source before display. The `Barlow Fallback` finding is documentation drift rather than a rendered defect. The two 2px side-tab borders in donation and our-work violate the stated visual rule; remaining findings are mostly undocumented colors, off-ramp type sizes and a radius mismatch.

No mutable browser tab was available, so no live overlay was injected. Fresh captures covered the homepage at 1440, 1200, 810 and 390px; builds, H1s, internal links, image alts and metadata were valid. Axe passes are useful evidence, but its configuration does not cover color contrast, heading order or label/content-name mismatch.

## Overall impression

The redesign is meaningfully closer to a client-ready site than the earlier critique described. It now gives adoption a visible protagonist and lets photography carry conversion. Do not restart a broad redesign. Finish the small, explicit delivery gate below.

## What's working

- Turrón is a persuasive named hero with a direct profile action and a quieter all-dogs route; current dogs appear before organizational exposition.
- Real photography does the emotional work without invented drama. The roster and fit module make adoption concrete rather than abstract.
- The responsive hierarchy remains legible at 390px and no horizontal clipping was observed in reviewed captures.

## Priority issues

### [P1] The visual grammar is over-applied

**Why it matters:** nearly every section competes at poster intensity, weakening route distinction and calm reassurance.

**Fix:** reserve hard black shadows, magenta underlines and saturated bands for dog moments and decisive conversion points. Let informational and trust sections use calmer space, simpler typography and fewer decorative rules.

**Suggested command:** `/impeccable quieter`

### [P1] The mobile consent banner obstructs the adoption story

**Why it matters:** it overlays the roster and becomes a stronger visual object than the dog cards on first visit.

**Fix:** keep explicit equal-weight accept/reject choices, but reduce its footprint, tighten the copy and ensure it never masks a card action.

**Suggested command:** `/impeccable polish`

### [P1] Branded 404 recovery is unproven

**Why it matters:** fresh capture of `/404` showed Astro's trailing-slash diagnostic rather than the branded recovery. This is inconclusive, but must be verified before handoff.

**Fix:** test an unknown preview/deployment-like URL and confirm the branded 404 offers current dogs, home and contact exits.

**Suggested command:** `/impeccable harden`

### [P2] Homepage pacing needs one calmer success transition

**Why it matters:** the story order is correct, but the dense orange donation block and large white gap before Praga weaken the emotional landing.

**Fix:** complete the Praga section as a composed photo-and-proof transition and remove redundant accents around it.

**Suggested command:** `/impeccable layout`

### [P2] Donation still makes visitors infer the recommended method

**Why it matters:** a skeptical donor has to work through a document-like route to distinguish transfer from monthly support and to find proof.

**Fix:** state the primary method plainly, put dated proof adjacent to it, and keep the alternate route visibly secondary.

**Suggested command:** `/impeccable clarify`
