## Overall Impression

The bones are strong: best-in-sector copy voice, real trust engineering on donar, and a homepage redesign direction that is genuinely distinctive. The crater is that this direction exists on exactly one page. Everything else is the pre-redesign site, and the collision between dialects — not any single page — is what reads as amateur. The single biggest opportunity: declare the home dialect THE system and migrate every page to it, ruthlessly.

## What's Working

1. **Copy voice.** "No necesitas tener todo resuelto antes de escribir", the radically honest donation disclaimer, "Si ocurre una urgencia, no la enfrentas sola" — specific, warm, zero guilt. Strongest asset on the site.
2. **The WIP home direction** (home-redesign.css): rotated bordered photo cards, hard shadows, 3-panel cyan action band, full-bleed Praga proof split. Distinctive AND inside the brand system.
3. **Trust engineering on donar:** verified account card, per-row copy buttons, "Revisada el {date}", visible RUT — better donation UX than most professional NGOs.

## Priority Issues

**[P0] One site, four dialects.** The home redesign language exists only on index.astro; every other page uses PageHero + RainbowDivider + eyebrows, and donar/hogar use a third grammar. *Why it matters:* cross-page inconsistency is exactly what reads as "amateur" — a visitor moving hero→profile→donar experiences three different sites. *Fix:* promote the home dialect sitewide — retire PageHero+RainbowDivider as a pairing; make the bordered hard-shadow card the only card primitive (dog-card, story-card, supporter-card, blog-card); one link grammar (magenta-underline text link = navigation, orange fill = conversion, black-border white = secondary); delete pale-cyan `.btn--secondary`, which reads as disabled next to the neubrutalist buttons. *Command:* /impeccable polish (system migration) after /impeccable shape.

**[P0] Banned patterns on four pages: numbered scaffolding + hero-metric grid.** *Why it matters:* it is the template grammar the brand explicitly rejects ("ONG distante, institucional o genérica"), sitting on the trust and contact pages. *Fix:* one canonical process component (the home ProcessStepper) sitewide; convert stat walls into evidence-as-links ("21 historias publicadas → véanlas"). A rescue org's numbers are dogs; show dogs, link the claim. *Command:* /impeccable distill + polish.

**[P1] Magenta and cyan have no jobs.** Magenta currently means: foster band, need panel, section divider, bank-data background, urgent note, tail CTA, expectations panel. Dark-on-magenta text in nuestro-trabajo is near-illegible (likely WCAG failure — confirm with axe). *Fix:* enforce a color contract — magenta = the dog's need / emotional beat only; cyan = the org acting only; orange = conversion fill only, never a panel; max one saturated band per page. Put the bank card on white with 2px black border + hard shadow — it will look MORE trustworthy. *Command:* /impeccable colorize (contract enforcement) or quieter.

**[P1] The conversion peak is the weakest moment.** Dog profile gives the decision two near-equal buttons plus Instagram plus share, and the primary punts to external Google Forms — inverting PRODUCT.md's stated conversion (start a conversation). *Fix:* one committed panel: orange "Escríbenos por {name}" → prefilled WhatsApp as primary; form demoted to secondary; a three-line "Qué pasa después" micro-timeline with expected response window underneath. *Command:* /impeccable shape (profile CTA block).

**[P1] nuestro-trabajo needs a rethink, not a reskin.** The trust page is the least trustworthy-feeling page: template hero, stat grid, unreadable magenta band, three-button ending. *Fix:* rebuild as "Las seis" — the six women who run the operation, with names/photos or one group photo, first-person narrative of how a case moves through their WhatsApp, then evidence-as-links. Boldest cheap win on the site. *Command:* /impeccable craft.

**[P2] Mobile hero + cookie banner collision.** At 390px the fixed cookie banner covers the hero lead and CTA on first paint; the statement card's -4.75rem overlap crops the dog's face ~60%. *Fix:* compact single-line mobile banner; full-height photo with statement below on mobile. *Command:* /impeccable adapt.

**[P2] donar information architecture.** 7,400px tall at 390px; the reference-amounts grid duplicates the impact section; the closing cyan panel introduces three MORE options after the decision. *Fix:* cut to three beats — bank card, monthly aside, one "dónde va" strip; move materials/services to contacto. *Command:* /impeccable distill.

**[P3] 404 breaks its own typography.** Only page whose h1 isn't condensed uppercase display; orange illustration panel violates the color contract. *Command:* /impeccable polish.

## Questions to Consider
